Leaders in A320 and B737 Flight Simulator Training

Formed in 2019, Arify Head Office is located in Sligo Regional Airport, We are Sligos first and only Flight Simulator Training Provider for Airline Pilots operating an Airbus A320 and Boeing 737 aircraft. Offering quality training to both qualified and traineed pilots. We also provide am chance to experience flight in out simulators to the public to attract more to this ever growing industry.

We have invested heavily in our A320 and B737 Training Simulators, which are wholly owned by us and been FNPT II Standards.We have full ECAM malfunction suit that can induce the same failures as that of a Full motion Simulator.

The Simulators have real Aribus Sidestick for the A320 and a Yoke for the B737, adjustable seats, map reading lights and all comforting features of the real aricraft.We are open for the public for limited experiences and events.
