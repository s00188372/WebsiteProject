Message

We are Aityfy, our Head offcie is located in Sligo, we are Ireland's leading flight sumulator provider for Airline Pilots operating an Airbus A320 and Boeing 737 Simulator.Simulation offering training support to both qualified, trainee pilots and the public. We are planning to expand our flight simulators in the future.

We have heavily invested into our Airbus and Beoing training simulators which meet ESEA FNPT II standards.We have full ECAM malfunction system that induces the same failures as that of a Full motion Level D Simulator.Our Simulators can also emmulate smoke in cockpit for smoke removal training.

Our Simulators have adjustable seats, tray tables, map reading lights, Dual linked rudder pedals, Ipads with Charts, an instructor station with touch screen and two further observer jumpseats. We also provide smoke in the cockpit simulation with breathing masks. Headsets are available to use and pilots are welcome to use their own.
